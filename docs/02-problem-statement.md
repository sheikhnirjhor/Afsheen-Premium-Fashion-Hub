
# 02. Problem Statement

## 1. Background
Modern luxury fashion shoppers demand fast, personalized, and visually striking online stores. Standard e-commerce platforms often feel impersonal and difficult to navigate when seeking specific styling guidance.

## 2. Core Problem
Customers struggle to match outfits, find personalized style recommendations, and navigate cluttered e-commerce interfaces without human fashion consultation.

## 3. Proposed Solution
Afsheen Premium Fashion Hub integrates an interactive AI Fashion Advisor (Google GenAI) alongside a sleek UI, offering immediate outfit pairings and tailored product suggestions.
