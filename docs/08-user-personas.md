# 08. User Personas

### Persona A: Sarah (Luxury Shopper)
* **Demographics:** Age 29, Corporate Professional.
* **Goals:** Quick shopping for luxury attire with instant styling guidance.
* **Pain Points:** Lack of personal styling assistants in standard online shops.

### Persona B: David (Trend Enthusiast)
* **Demographics:** Age 24, Creative Director.
* **Goals:** Explore new seasonal arrivals on a mobile-first interface.
